Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner for Studying in South Korea

Korizm Global Education is a student-first consultancy dedicated to helping you study at top universities across South Korea. We believe the path to Korea should be clear, transparent, and stress-free — not a maze of paperwork and guesswork.

Our experienced counsellors understand the Korean education system inside out — from admissions and TOPIK requirements to D-2 student visas and scholarships like GKS. We craft a personalised plan that maximises your chances of admission, and we walk with you from your first question to your first day on a Korean campus.

  • Korea-focused, experienced counsellors
  • Honest, no-pressure guidance
  • End-to-end support, start to finish
  • Strong network of Korean universities

Our Services

Everything You Need, Under One Roof

From shortlisting Korean universities to settling into life in Korea, our team supports you at every milestone of your journey to South Korea.

University Selection

We match you with Korean universities and programmes that fit your profile, budget, and career goals — no generic lists.

Admission Guidance

Study plans, essays, recommendation letters and applications — we polish every detail to make your file stand out to Korean admissions teams.

Student Visa Assistance

D-2 and D-4 Korean student visa documentation, financial proofs and interview prep handled by experts — with a 98% visa success record.

Scholarships & Funding

From GKS to university and TOPIK-based scholarships, we identify the funding you qualify for and help you apply the right way.

Pre-Departure Support

Flights, dormitory and accommodation help, packing checklists and Korean culture briefings so you arrive prepared and confident.

Post-Arrival Care

Airport pickup, alien registration, Korean bank account, SIM cards and a friendly local network so you settle into Korea without the stress.
